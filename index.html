The Solar System

Their are 8 planets in our solar system. 9 planets if you decide to include Pluto. The solar system, that is about 4.5 billion kilometers, encompasses the 8 Planets in this order. Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune. It also has other celestial objects like natural satellites (i.e the Moon), asteroids, comets, planetoids, and more!
To read more, check out NASA's webpage to learn more about our solar system!
